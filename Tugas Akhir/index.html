Aircraft Gallery

Dassault Falcon, a division of Dassault Aviation, is renowned for its exceptional business jets. The roots of Dassault Falcon trace back to legendary fighter jets like the Mirage and Rafale. These fighter aircraft have left an indelible mark in aviation history.

Every Falcon business jet inherits technologies proven in the fiercest environments, thanks to its fighter jet DNA. The combination of this heritage and cutting-edge technology ensures that nothing flies or feels quite like a Falcon.

Since 1963, Falcon business jets have rewritten aviation history in terms of technology, passenger experience, and style. Dassault's story continues to unfold, and confident that history will repeat itself.
